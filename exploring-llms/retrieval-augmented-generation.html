Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG)

Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) is a natural language processing (NLP) technique that uses semantic search over a corpus of documents to enhance and localize the use of generative-based large language models (LLM). RAG is often used to improve the accuracy and reliability of LLMs by providing a better method for contextualizing the generative abilities of these models.
